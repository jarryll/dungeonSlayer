Push "Start" to begin!

Welcome, brave adventurer!

Kill as many monsters as you can in 60 seconds, but beware of their attacks.

In this game:

  • Physical Attacks win against Sneak Attacks
  • Magical Attacks win against Physical Attacks
  • Sneak Attacks win against Magical Attacks

Press "A" for Physical Attack , "S" for Magical Attack

or "D" for Sneak Attack!
